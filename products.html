Our Banana Products: Peel Into the Fun!

Carefully crafted with a dash of humor – these products are a bunch above the rest!

Fresh Bananas

So fresh they might even tell you a banana peel joke – guaranteed to brighten your day!

Available in Cavendish, Red, Lady Finger, and Plantain varieties, our bananas are perfect for snacking, baking, or making smoothies. All of our bananas are certified organic and ethically sourced.

Dried Bananas

Our dried bananas: chewy, charming, and with a sun‑kissed sense of humor!

Our sun-dried bananas retain all the natural flavors and nutrients of fresh bananas in a convenient, shelf-stable form. Perfect for hiking, lunchboxes, or as a healthy snack alternative.

Banana Chips

Crunchy, zesty, and so addictive you might find yourself peeling for more – no split-second regrets!

Available in original, honey glazed, chocolate dipped, and spicy varieties. Our banana chips are kettle-cooked in small batches to ensure perfect crispness and flavor in every bite.

Banana Flour

Gluten-free and guaranteed to make you smile – don’t be such a stick in the mud!

High in resistant starch and perfect for baking, our banana flour can replace traditional wheat flour in most recipes. It's an excellent option for those following gluten-free, paleo, or clean-eating diets.

Banana Plants

Bring home your very own banana plant – guaranteed to be the life (and peel) of your party!

We offer several varieties suitable for different climates, including indoor cultivation. Each plant comes with detailed care instructions to help you successfully grow bananas in your home garden.

Banana Merchandise

From tees to totes, our merch lets you show off your fruity flair with a cheeky twist.

From banana-themed t-shirts and tote bags to kitchen accessories and home decor, our merchandise collection has something for every banana enthusiast. All items feature our playful banana designs.
